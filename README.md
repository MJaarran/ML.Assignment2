1. Overview
This dataset consists of historical sales records from a supermarket company, covering three different branches over a three-month period. It serves as a foundation for data quality assessment, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and preprocessing for machine learning tasks.
2. Dataset Structure
Total Observations: 1,000 rows.

Total Attributes: 17 columns.

Column Definitions
Invoice ID: Unique identification number for each sales slip.

Branch: Branch identifier (e.g., Alex, Giza).

City: Location of the supermarket.

Customer type: Type of customer (Member or Normal).

Gender: Gender of the customer.

Product line: Category of the item purchased.

Unit price: Price of each product in USD.

Quantity: Number of items purchased.

Tax 5%: 5% tax fee on the purchase.

Total (Sales): Total cost including tax.

Date: Date of the transaction.

Time: Time of the transaction.

Payment: Payment method (Cash, Credit card, or Ewallet).

COGS: Cost of goods sold.

Gross margin percentage: Margin percentage.

Gross income: Gross income from the sale.

Rating: Customer experience rating (Scale 1–10).
